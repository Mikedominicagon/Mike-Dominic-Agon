WHAT IS CHEATING?

Cheating in school robs everyone involved. The cheater,

their peers, and the teacher are all deprived of the

full benefits of education. When a student is chasing

higher grades or feeling overwhelmed by the school workload,

the temptation to cheat will appear, and it's the job of

the student to resist that temptation.
